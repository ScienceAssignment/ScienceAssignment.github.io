Coal

How is coal made?

The coal is supplied into a giant boiler where they heat up water. This water is then used to speed up a turbine that creates energy.

Advantages Disadvantages
Their is an abundance of Coal in many Contries It's non-renewable
It's a full time fuel and can run constantly It's one of the biggest emitters of CO2

How much energy is produced

Total energy produced according to Forbes:

For every kilogram of coal that gets burned, a total of 2.312 × 107 Joules of energy gets released, meaning we need to burn a total of 24 billion tonnes of coal in order to meet Earth's energy needs. - Forbes
