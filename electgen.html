Cosumption & Production per State[1]

According to the 2019 Australian Energy Update: Over 25% of Australia's Energy Consumption was consumed by New South Wales and the ACT followed by Queensland with 24.9% and Victoria with 21.6%. If you combined NSW, The ACT, WA, VIC and Queensland's energy Consumption it would make up 91.2% of the energy Consumption.

Cosumption per Sector

According to the 2019 Australian Energy Update: 28.1% of goes to transportation followed by Electricity Supply(Houses and small buisness) at 26.3% and Manafacturing at 17.4%. Together this makes 71.8% of energy consumption
